Shipping Policy

Shipping Policy

At LocalMart, we strive to deliver your orders promptly and efficiently through our trusted local sellers. Below is our shipping policy:

  • Processing Time: Orders are processed within 1-3 business days after payment confirmation. Sellers may require additional time for custom or handmade items.
  • Shipping Methods: Shipping options vary by seller and location. Common methods include standard (3-7 days), expedited (1-3 days), and local pickup (where available).
  • Shipping Costs: Costs are calculated at checkout based on weight, dimensions, and destination. Some sellers offer free shipping on orders above a certain amount.
  • Local Delivery: For customers within the seller’s area, same-day or next-day delivery may be available. Check product listings for details.
  • Tracking: Once shipped, you’ll receive a tracking number via email (if applicable). Log into your LocalMart account to track your order.
  • International Shipping: Limited international shipping is available. Contact the seller directly for options and costs.
  • Undeliverable Packages: If a package is returned due to an incorrect address or failure to claim, the buyer is responsible for additional shipping fees for reshipment.

For specific shipping inquiries, contact the seller through your LocalMart account or email us at support@localmart.com.

Returns & Refunds

Returns & Refunds

We want you to be satisfied with your LocalMart purchase. Here’s our returns and refunds policy:

  • Eligibility: Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery in unused, original condition with all tags and packaging intact.
  • Non-Returnable Items: Perishable goods, custom-made products, and personal care items are non-returnable unless damaged or defective.
  • Return Process: Initiate a return through your LocalMart account. Contact the seller to arrange return shipping. Buyers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or incorrect.
  • Refunds: Once the seller receives and inspects the returned item, a refund will be issued to the original payment method within 5-10 business days. Shipping fees are non-refundable.
  • Damaged or Defective Items: Report issues within 48 hours of delivery with photos. The seller will provide a replacement or refund, including return shipping costs.
  • Disputes: If you and the seller cannot resolve a return issue, contact LocalMart support at support@localmart.com for assistance.

Please review the seller’s specific return policy on the product listing, as some may have additional terms.
